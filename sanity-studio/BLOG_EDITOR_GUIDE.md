# 📝 Blog Post Editor Guide

Quick reference for creating blog posts in Sanity Studio.

---

## 📸 Featured Image Requirements

### **Recommended Size**
- **Dimensions**: 1200 × 675 pixels
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **Format**: JPG or WebP
- **File Size**: Under 250KB

### **Why These Dimensions?**
This size works perfectly for:
- ✅ Blog listing cards
- ✅ Blog post hero images
- ✅ Social media sharing (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn)
- ✅ All screen sizes (mobile, tablet, desktop)

### **Image Optimization Tools**
Before uploading, optimize your images:
- **TinyPNG**: https://tinypng.com/
- **Squoosh**: https://squoosh.app/
- **ImageOptim** (Mac): https://imageoptim.com/

### **Composition Tips**
- ✅ Keep important content **centered**
- ✅ Use **high contrast** images
- ✅ Avoid text on images (may be cropped)
- ✅ Consider the dark gradient overlay on cards

---

## ✍️ Writing Guidelines

### **Title**
- Keep under 60 characters for best SEO
- Make it clear and engaging
- ✅ Good: "5 Ways AI is Transforming Public Transportation"
- ❌ Bad: "Blog Post 1"

### **Excerpt**
- 150-200 characters is ideal
- This appears on blog cards and search results
- Make it compelling to encourage clicks

### **Content**
- Use headings (H2, H3) to structure your content
- Add lists, quotes, and links for engagement
- Break up long paragraphs
- Average reading speed: 200-250 words per minute

### **Reading Time**
Calculate based on word count:
- 500 words ≈ 2-3 minutes
- 1000 words ≈ 4-5 minutes
- 1500 words ≈ 6-7 minutes
- 2000 words ≈ 8-10 minutes

---

## 🏷️ Categories & Tags

### **Categories**
- Select 1-3 categories per post
- Categories help organize content
- Readers can filter by category

### **Tags**
- Add 5-10 relevant keywords
- Press Enter after each tag
- Examples: "AI", "Transportation", "Mobile Apps"
- Tags improve searchability

---

## 📅 Publishing

### **Status Options**
- **📝 Draft**: Work in progress (not visible on website)
- **✅ Published**: Live and visible to readers
- **📦 Archived**: Hidden but kept for reference

### **Published Date**
- Set to current date/time for immediate publishing
- Set to future date to schedule publication
- Can be backdated if needed

### **Featured Posts**
- Check "Featured Post" to highlight on homepage
- Only feature your best content
- Limit to 3-5 featured posts at a time

---

## ♿ Accessibility

### **Alt Text**
- Required for all images
- Describe what's in the image
- 10-125 characters
- ✅ Good: "Team collaborating on transportation software dashboard"
- ❌ Bad: "Image" or "Photo"

---

## 🔍 SEO Best Practices

### **Meta Title**
- 50-60 characters
- Include main keyword
- Make it compelling

### **Meta Description**
- 150-160 characters
- Summarize the post
- Include call-to-action

### **Keywords**
- Add 5-10 relevant keywords
- Separate with commas
- Focus on search intent

---

## ✅ Pre-Publish Checklist

Before publishing, verify:

- [ ] Featured image uploaded (1200 × 675px)
- [ ] Alt text added to image
- [ ] Title is clear and under 60 characters
- [ ] Excerpt is compelling (150-200 characters)
- [ ] Content is well-structured with headings
- [ ] Reading time is accurate
- [ ] Author is selected
- [ ] 1-3 categories selected
- [ ] Tags added
- [ ] Published date is set
- [ ] Status is set to "Published"
- [ ] SEO metadata filled (optional but recommended)

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Write in batches** - Draft multiple posts, then publish on schedule
2. **Use templates** - Create a content template for consistency
3. **Preview before publishing** - Check how it looks on the website
4. **Update old posts** - Keep content fresh and relevant
5. **Monitor performance** - Track which posts perform best
6. **Engage with readers** - Respond to comments and feedback
