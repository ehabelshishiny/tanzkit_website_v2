Tranzkit.com Website Structure & Content Requirements

This document outlines the full website structure for Tranzkit.com, including every page, section, and what content is required for each. It is designed to help content writers understand exactly what to deliver for every part of the site. All main pages and most sections require bilingual content (English first, Arabic below).

1. Home Page
Hero Section
Bilingual headline, sub-headline, and call-to-action (CTA) introducing Tranzkit’s value proposition.
What’s needed: 1–2 lines for each, in both English and Arabic.
Overview Section
Short introductory paragraph summarizing the platform and 3 key features or values.
What’s needed: 1 paragraph + 3 bullet points (EN/AR).
Features Section
Short Intro
What’s needed: Feature title + 1–2 lines per feature (EN/AR).
Logo Bar
Partner/client logos, subheader, 2–3 paragraphs about credibility, 3–5 feature bullets, CTA.
What’s needed: Subheader, 2–3 short paragraphs, 3–5 bullets, CTA (EN/AR).
Testimonials
Quotes from clients, subheader, 2–3 paragraphs, 3–5 feature bullets, CTA.
What’s needed: Subheader, 2–3 short paragraphs, 3–5 bullets, CTA (EN/AR).
Call-to-Action
Strong CTA, subheader, 2–3 paragraphs, 3–5 feature bullets.
What’s needed: Subheader, 2–3 short paragraphs, 3–5 bullets, CTA (EN/AR).

2. Solutions Page
Hero Section
Headline, subheadline, CTA (EN/AR).
Overview Section
Intro paragraph, 3 clickable cards (For Drivers & Passengers, For Enterprises, For Transport Operators).
What’s needed: 1 paragraph, 3 card titles + 1–2 lines each (EN/AR).
Detailed Sections (each card leads to a section/page):
For Drivers & Passengers: Subheader, 2–3 paragraphs, 3–5 feature bullets, CTA.
For Enterprises: Subheader, 2–3 paragraphs, 3–5 feature bullets, CTA.
For Transport Operators: Subheader, 2–3 paragraphs, 3–5 feature bullets, CTA.
What’s needed: For each, subheader, 2–3 short paragraphs, 3–5 bullets, CTA (EN/AR).
Call-to-Action
Unified CTA to engage/book demo (EN/AR).

3. Resources Page
Hero Section
Headline, subheadline, CTA (EN/AR).
Overview Section
Purpose of the Resources Hub, 3 key focuses (Knowledge, Insights, Support).
What’s needed: 1 paragraph, 3 bullets (EN/AR).
Detailed Sections (each is a subpage/section):
Help Center / Documentation: Subheader, 2–3 paragraphs, 3–5 bullets, CTA. Dynamic: App Guide (template for each app/feature).
Webinars / Videos: Subheader, 2–3 paragraphs, 3–5 bullets, CTA. Dynamic: Video/Webinar page template.
Blog: Subheader, 2–3 paragraphs, 3–5 bullets, CTA. Dynamic: Blog post template.
Whitepapers / eBooks: Subheader, 2–3 paragraphs, 3–5 bullets, CTA. Dynamic: Whitepaper/eBook template.
Case Studies: Subheader, 2–3 paragraphs, 3–5 bullets, CTA. Dynamic: Case Study template.
FAQ: Subheader, 2–3 paragraphs, 3–5 bullets, CTA. Dynamic: FAQ item template (grouped by category).
What’s needed: For each, subheader, 2–3 short paragraphs, 3–5 bullets, CTA (EN/AR), plus dynamic templates.
Call-to-Action
Final CTA to engage/subscribe (EN/AR).

Instructions for Writers:
Each bullet is a required section or dynamic template.
For each section: prepare subheader, 2–3 paragraphs, 3–5 feature bullets, and a CTA (all bilingual).
Dynamic nodes mean you’ll need templates for repeated content (e.g., blog posts, FAQs).
